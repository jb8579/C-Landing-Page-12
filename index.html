How Sweepstakes Games Work

Sweepstakes games operate on a free-to-play or low-risk model, allowing players to acquire virtual currency or tokens that act as their entries into the games. This setup makes sweepstakes distinct from gambling, as players are not required to stake real money. Instead, players use these entries to access a wide range of games, from slots to scratch cards, where they can win real prizes.

Players receive these virtual coins through daily bonuses, promotional offers, or optional purchases of items that grant additional entries. By using certified Random Number Generators (RNGs) to ensure fair play, sweepstakes games offer an engaging experience while meeting the transparency expectations of U.S. players. Any winnings from sweepstakes can be redeemed as cash or other prizes, depending on the platform's policies.
